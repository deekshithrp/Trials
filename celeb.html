Prabhu deva


Introduction:Prabhu deva is an Indian dance choreographer, director and also an actor[1] who is also known as Micheal jackson of India. He has worked in Kannada, Tamil, Telugu and hindi industry.

Dancing carrer: He entered to this feild when he was of age 13. Prabhu deva has choreographed for many Indian superstars since then, He started learning bharatanatyam. Since he was a fast learner his teacher taught him within a months time. He went on to clear all the exams. Later sometime he came across a casette of Micheal Jackson and wanted to explore the new genre of dance.

Personal life:He was born near a small village named DOORA[2]which is around 15kms from Mysore. He married to Latha and they got divorced in 2011. He then married to Himani in 2020. He lost his elder son who was aged 13 due to cancer and that remains a tragedy for him.
